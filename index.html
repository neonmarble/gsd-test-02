Years Operating

Founded in 2012, headquartered in Yangon

What began as a single classroom has grown into a full-scale maritime academy with dedicated firefighting grounds, lifeboat stations, and bridge simulator facilities.

Training Programs

Comprehensive Courses for Every Stage of Your Maritime Career

From foundational safety training to specialized officer-level certification, our programs meet IMO Model Course standards and are recognized by flag states across the globe.

5 Days Foundation

STCW Basic Safety Training (BST)

The essential entry credential for all seafarers. Covers Personal Survival Techniques, Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting, Elementary First Aid, Personal Safety & Social Responsibilities, and Security Awareness. STCW Reg. VI/1 compliant.

STCW VI/1 Learn More →
4 Days Advanced

Advanced Fire Fighting (AFF)

Intensive practical training in command-level firefighting operations. Includes structural fire behavior, SCBA deployment in smoke-filled environments, and fire party organization. STCW Reg. VI/3 compliant.

STCW VI/3 Learn More →
5 Days Specialized

Tanker Familiarization (Oil & Chemical)

Specialized training for seafarers assigned to oil and chemical tankers. Covers cargo properties, tank atmosphere control, pollution prevention, and safety management systems. STCW Reg. V/1-1 compliant.

STCW V/1-1 Learn More →
10 Days Specialized

GMDSS General Operator Certificate (GOC)

Comprehensive instruction in the Global Maritime Distress and Safety System. Trainees master satellite communications, digital selective calling, NAVTEX, EPIRBs, and SARTs. Required for all deck officers.

STCW IV/2 Learn More →
4 Days Advanced

Proficiency in Survival Craft & Rescue Boats (PSCRB)

Hands-on lifeboat command and launching operations under realistic conditions. Trainees manage evacuation sequences, operate davit systems, and coordinate rescue boat maneuvers. STCW Reg. VI/2 compliant.

STCW VI/2 Learn More →
3 Days Specialized

Ship Security Officer (SSO)

Prepares designated security officers to perform ship security assessments, implement the Ship Security Plan, and coordinate with Port Facility Security Officers under the ISPS Code. STCW Reg. VI/5 compliant.

STCW VI/5 • ISPS Learn More →
5 Days Advanced

ECDIS & Bridge Resource Management

Equips deck officers with the skills to operate Electronic Chart Display and Information Systems and apply resource management principles on the bridge. Includes simulator-based collision avoidance exercises.

STCW II/1 • IMO 1.27 Learn More →
