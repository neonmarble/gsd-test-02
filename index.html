Our Training Pillars

Comprehensive, standards-driven curricula designed to prepare seafarers for real-world operational challenges.

Bridge Simulation

Full-mission bridge simulators with RADAR, ECDIS, and GMDSS integration. Train in high-fidelity scenarios from congested ports to heavy weather navigation.

Engine Room Operations

Hands-on engine room simulator training covering main propulsion, auxiliary systems, and emergency response. STCW-compliant for all engine room ratings.

Leadership & Management

Maritime resource management, crisis leadership, and regulatory compliance. Courses aligned with IMO Model Courses 1.39 and 1.22 for senior officers.

Training Infrastructure

State-of-the-art simulation suites providing immersive, risk-free training environments.

Engine Control Simulator

GMDSS Radio Suite
