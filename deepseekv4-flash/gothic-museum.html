COLLECTION HIGHLIGHTS

TREASURES OF THE AGES

🕯

THE RELIQUARY OF SAINT CECILIA

A 13th-century gilded silver reliquary containing what is believed to be a fragment of the saint's veil. Adorned with 47 gemstones including sapphires, rubies, and emeralds.

📖

THE BLACK HOURS

An extraordinarily rare book of hours painted entirely on vellum stained black, with text and illuminations in gold and silver leaf. Created c. 1460 in Bruges.

⚔

THE CRUSADER'S PSALTER

A psalter carried by a Knight Templar during the Third Crusade. Margins contain rare marginalia blending Christian iconography with Islamic geometric patterns.

👑

THE CROWN OF SHADOWS

A processional crown from the Kingdom of Georgia, forged from darkened silver and set with garnets and amethysts. Said to have been worn during night liturgies.
