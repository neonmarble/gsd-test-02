OUR STORY

Born from the Aegean, raised by the sun.

Thalassa began as a fisherman's taverna on the white cliffs of Santorini in 1982. Yiannis and Elena Karamanlis served whatever the sea provided that morning — grilled octopus, caught by hand, drizzled with local olive oil and wild oregano from the hills.

Four decades later, our family has grown to three islands, but the philosophy remains unchanged: honour the ingredient, respect the tradition, feed the soul. No shortcuts, no compromises, just honest Mediterranean cooking.
