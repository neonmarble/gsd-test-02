Our Training Pillars

Advanced curriculum designed for offshore operations, subsea engineering, and extreme environment readiness.

Dynamic Positioning Systems

DP induction through advanced unlimited certification. Train on Kongsberg K-Pos and Veripos systems with real-time failure injection scenarios from power loss to thruster walkout.

Subsea Engineering & ROV

Remotely operated vehicle piloting, subsea survey operations, pipeline inspection, and intervention tooling. Train with real ROV consoles and subsea simulation databases.

Arctic & Extreme Operations

Ice navigation, polar code compliance, cold-weather survival, and operations in high latitudes. Includes simulator scenarios in modeled Arctic and Antarctic conditions.
