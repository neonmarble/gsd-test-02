Our Training Pillars

Master the art and science of navigation through our comprehensive, IMO-compliant curriculum.

Electronic Navigation Systems

Advanced ECDIS training with type-specific simulators from Transas, Sperry, and Furuno. Master route planning, real-time AIS integration, and AI-powered collision avoidance systems.

Celestial & Terrestrial Navigation

Blend classical sextant navigation with modern GPS methodologies. Learn position fixing by stars, sun running fixes, and contingency navigation when electronic systems fail.

Integrated Bridge Systems

Holistic training on integrated bridge platforms combining RADAR, ECDIS, conning displays, and DP systems. Learn multi-system coordination for safe, efficient vessel operations.
