Our Training Pillars

A curriculum forged by a century of maritime tradition, refined with modern precision.

Classical Seamanship

Knotwork, rigging, watchkeeping discipline, and traditional navigation. Foundations that build character and competence before technology.

Command & Leadership

Maritime resource management, crew welfare, flag state compliance, and bridge team dynamics. Shaping officers into captains.

International Regulations

COLREGS mastery, SOLAS compliance, MARPOL conventions, and ISM Code auditing. The legal framework every officer must command.
