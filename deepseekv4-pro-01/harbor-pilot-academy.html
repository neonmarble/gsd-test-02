Our Training Pillars

Three core disciplines that transform experienced mariners into elite harbor pilots.

Advanced Ship Handling

Master squat effect, bank interaction, and passing vessel dynamics. Train on 1:25 scale manned models in our purpose-built lake facility under real wind and current conditions.

Tug & Escort Operations

Coordinated multi-tug maneuvers, indirect towing, escort operations in narrow fairways, and emergency arrest scenarios. Train with active duty tug masters.

Risk Assessment & Decision Making

BRM for pilots, dynamic risk assessment matrices, human factors in pilotage, and real-time decision-making under pressure. Based on IMO Resolution A.960.

Training Infrastructure

Purpose-built facilities designed to replicate real-world port environments with zero operational risk.

Manned Model Lake

13-acre lake, 1:25 scale VLCC & container vessel models

Pilotage Bridge Simulator

360° projection, 12 port models, live AIS feed

Tug Command Simulator

Voith, ASD, and conventional tug physics

Portable Pilot Unit Lab

PPU training on all major manufacturer systems
