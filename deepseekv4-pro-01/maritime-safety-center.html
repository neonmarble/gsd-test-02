Our Training Pillars

Three core competencies that save lives at sea. Every course meets or exceeds IMO, OPITO, and flag state requirements.

Advanced Firefighting

Live-fire exercises in our multi-level steel fire simulator. SCBA operations, search & rescue in zero visibility, compartment cooling, and team command under realistic heat and smoke conditions.

Survival Craft & Rescue

PSCRB and FRB training in our wave pool facility. Lifeboat launching, capsize recovery, helicopter rescue hoist drills, and mass evacuation coordination in simulated adverse weather.

Enclosed Space & Hazmat

Confined space entry, gas detection, SCBA donning under stress, casualty extraction, and hazmat containment. Realistic mock-ups of ballast tanks, cargo holds, and pump rooms.
